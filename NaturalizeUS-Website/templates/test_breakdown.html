What is the US Citizenship Test?

Breakdown

You will be tested on 3 different things:

Civics Knowledge and Comprehension

This will test your knowledge of American Civics and History. You will be asked to correctly answer 6 pout of 10 questions chosen from a set of 100 total questions. Be prepared to answer all the questions.

English Reading/Speaking Skills

Your fluency and understanding of the English will be observed by your USCIS agent during your eligibility interview. You will also be asked to read aloud 1 out of three sentences given during your interview.

English Writing Skills

Your writing ability in the English language will be tested by you correctly writing 1 of 3 sentences given to you orally by your USCIS agent.
